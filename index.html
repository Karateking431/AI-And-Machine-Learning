What is AI and Why It Matters

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It is basically when computers are made smart enough to think and learn like humans. They can understand information and help us do different tasks.

AI is important today because we use it in many things without even noticing. For example, apps, maps, and learning platforms use AI. It helps save time and makes things easier for people

Stages of AI & Generative AI

Narrow AI

Narrow AI is designed to do only one task.Examples include Google Translate and Face ID.

General AI

General AI is a future idea where AI can think like humans.This type of AI does not exist yet.

Super AI

Super AI is only an imagination.It is AI that is smarter than humans.

Generative AI

Generative AI can create text, images, and music.Examples include chatbots and AI art
