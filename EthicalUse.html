Using AI the smart, fair, and responsible way

Do’s and Don’ts of Using AI

Do’s of AI Don’ts of AI
Use AI to learn and understand topics better Do not copy AI answers and submit them
Use AI to boost creativity and ideas Do not use AI for cheating in exams
Use AI as help when you are stuck Do not create or spread fake content

Bias and Misinformation

AI can sometimes give wrong or biased information. This happens because AI learns from human-made data,and humans can make mistakes. AI does not always know what is fair or true, so its answers should be checked before trusting them.Also, AI cannot think or understand situations like humans do, so it may miss context or common sense, making human judgment important before using its information.

Why Human Thinking Is Still Important

AI cannot think like humans. It does not understand emotions, values, or real-life situations. Human thinking is important to make decisions, check facts, and use common sense. People can understand right and wrong, consider consequences, and adapt to new situations in ways AI cannot. AI should help humans by supporting learning and problem-solving, not replace human thinking or decision-making.
