Natural Language Processing (NLP)

What is NLP?

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a branch of artificial intelligence that focuses on how computers interact with human language. It allows machines to read, listen to, understand, and respond to the way people naturally speak and write.

NLP helps computers work with text and spoken language, instead of numbers and codes. This means computers can understand emails, messages, voice commands, and documents written by humans.

How computers understand human language

Computers do not understand language naturally like humans. NLP helps computers by breaking language into clear steps and patterns:

  • Reads or hears the text entered by the user

  • Breaks sentences into words and symbols

  • Identifies important words (such as love, learning, AI)

  • Understands meaning and context of the sentence

  • Detects emotions or intent behind the words

  • Generates a suitable response based on understanding

    • These steps help computers respond in a way that feels natural to humans.
